First off, some definitions: 

- **Studygram** = study + instagram, a subculture on Instagram dedidicated to studying and taking notes in an organized and aesthetically pleasing manner. It was influenced by a corresponding subculture on Tumblr (e.g. my inactive [studyblr](https://gpa-saver.tumblr.com/)). 

- **Bullet Journal**, "bujo" for short, is a method of personal organization. The system organizes scheduling, reminders, to-do lists, brainstorming, and other organizational tasks into a single notebook. While traditional rules of bullet journaling are outlined by [Ryder Carroll](https://bulletjournal.com/pages/about), I find that bujo styles are deeply personal. 

On my studygram [@mathxmatics](https://www.instagram.com/mathxmatics/) I primarily post pages from my bullet journal and planner. I also get abundantly excited about trying new stationery and pens. Luckily, keeping journals gives me an excuse to use all my stickers, washi tapes, brush pens, highlighters, and inks that I hoard up in my office.
